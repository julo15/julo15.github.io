Apéritif & Amusements

The Gaming Salon
Games of chance and skill along the promenade. The house always wins; the prizes are nonetheless enormous and plush.A FEW COINS · DIGNITY OPTIONAL
Spook-A-Rama
A vintage 1955 immersive theatrical experience. Patrons are conveyed by carriage through scenes of curated dread.$10 · CONEY'S OLDEST DARK RIDE

The Grand Promenade

Eastward along the esplanade  ·  a digestive constitutional

The Parachute Jump
We process beneath Brooklyn's own Eiffel Tower (1939, no longer operational — neither are we) and the complimentary outdoor antiquities: a surviving Steeplechase horse, a Cyclops, the salt air.GRATIS · 2.7 MILES OF SEASIDE BOARDS
— III —

The Eurasian Degustation

Through Little Odessa, beneath the elevated railway  ·  the principal affair

Amuse-Bouche · Brighton Tandir
Hand-laminated Samarkand pastries, flame-kissed against the walls of a traditional clay hearth. The establishment maintains no website, no telephone, no listing — a discretion the truly elite will appreciate.$3–4 · CASH ONLY · PUMPKIN SAMSA AT NOON
The Principal Course · Toné Café
A molten vessel of farmer's cheese, finished tableside with a quivering yolk and a generous knob of butter, beside the city's oldest Georgian clay oven. Conversation is encouraged to breathe.~$14 · GEORGIAN · OUR HOUSE SELECTION
The Bazaar · Tashkent
An overwhelming hall of prepared delicacies, selected by gesture from the steam table among the regulars. Plov is borne ceremoniously to the boardwalk.BY WEIGHT · PRICELESS, NEARLY LITERALLY
Petits Fours · La Brioche
